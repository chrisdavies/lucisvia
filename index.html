Hi. Welcome.

We are a group of young adults (18-35 ish) in South Carolina who meet weekly to study God's word, pray, and fellowship. We also participate in charities, play sports, go to movies, and do a million other things.

Ignite your faith. Get plugged in.

Charleston

Wednesdays from 7 - 8:30 at The Daughters of St. Paul

Feel free to come a bit early. We are located around on the side of the daughters' building in the John Paul II room. (You'll see two big doors with glass windows.)

Mt. Pleasant

Mondays from 7 - 8:30 at Christ Our King

We meet at the Russell House, which is the little building beside the great big beach house.

Summerville

Thursdays from 7 - 8:30 at St. Leo University

The full address is 2430 Mall Drive, First Federal Corporate Center Suite 185 in North Charleston.
